What is the SaaS Growth Framework?

The SaaS Growth Framework is a comprehensive go-to-market playbook that combines proven growth models – from the Bullseye Framework for channel focus to RICE scoring for prioritization and Pirate Metrics (AARRR) for funnel optimization.

This framework helps B2B SaaS startups systematically identify, prioritize, and execute the right marketing and demand generation tactics for their stage and goals. It provides a detailed guide covering all key areas of marketing strategy development and execution.

Framework Methodology

The SaaS Growth Framework integrates multiple proven methodologies to create a unified approach to B2B SaaS marketing:

The Bullseye Framework

A structured approach to finding your most effective marketing channels. It visualizes all options as a target with three rings:

  • Outer Ring: Brainstorming all possible channels
  • Middle Ring: Testing the most promising ideas
  • Inner Ring (Bullseye): Focusing on the channel(s) that drive the most traction

This approach ensures you invest deeper only in channels with proven traction, rather than spreading yourself too thin.

RICE Prioritization

A quantitative scoring model to prioritize marketing initiatives, where RICE stands for:

  • Reach: How many people will this affect?
  • Impact: How much will it move the needle?
  • Confidence: How sure are you about the estimates?
  • Effort: How much work will it take?

This helps objectively compare different marketing opportunities and focus on high-impact, efficient activities.

Pirate Metrics (AARRR)

A framework to ensure your marketing covers the entire customer journey:

  • Acquisition: How users discover you
  • Activation: Their first positive experience
  • Retention: Repeat usage and engagement
  • Referral: Users recommending your product
  • Revenue: Monetization and growth

This ensures a balanced approach that doesn't just focus on top-of-funnel activities but nurtures users throughout their journey.
